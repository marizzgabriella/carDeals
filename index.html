Lamborghini V12 Vision Gran Turismo

Overview

The Lambo V12 Vision Gran Turismo is a concept car made by Lamborghini for the Vision Gran Turismo project. Launched at the 2019 FIA-Certified Gran Turismo Championships World Finals in Monaco, it was originally set to appear in Gran Turismo Sport as part of a future update, originally due to release in Spring 2020, but never made it. However, it made its debut in Gran Turismo 7.

Price: $478,000

2020 Rolls-Royce Cullinan Models

Overview

The Rolls-Royce Cullinan comes in two trim levels: Cullinan and Black Badge. All Cullinans are all-wheel-drive, use an eight-speed automatic transmission, and draw power from a 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine with two available levels of output. There are myriad stand-alone options, encompassing everything from an upgraded audio system to a gold-plated hood ornament, as well as endless options for interior and exterior color combinations.

Price Range: $249,850 to $309,950

Average Selling Price: $283,643

2020 Ferrari 812 GTS

Overview

The 2020 Ferrari 812 GTS is a high-performance grand tourer that combines stunning design with exceptional power and handling. It's a convertible version of the 812 Superfast, with a retractable hardtop that opens in just 14 seconds.

Price: $469,350
